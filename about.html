The ithaca harvest festival is an annual festival, now 37 years old, that celebrates the local community and they different apple delicacies they have to offer. The event includes a variety of food vendors whom mostly just take cash, and different artistic demonstrations such as music and dancing.

The event is coordianted by the Downtown Ithaca alliance in coordination with different sponsors in order to share the rich economy of local farmers and the apples they harvest.
