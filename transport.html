The TCAT can take you to various parts of Ithaca. Keep in mind that the TCAT is free for first years at Cornell (freshmen and tranfers) and is free to regular cornell students after 6pm on Weekdays and all day on Weekdays.There are also various apps to help with Bus time such as Ithaca Transit

Route 10

This Route operates Monday through Friday and goes to Cornell and then the Commons. This route goes through west and central campus.

Route 30

This Route operates Monday through Friday and goes to the airport, Cornell and then the Commons. This route goes through north and central campus.

Route 70

This Route operates Saturday and Sunday and goes to the airport, Cornell and then the Commons. This route goes through north and west campus

Route 72

This Route operates Saturday and Sunday and goes to the airport, Cornell and then the Commons. This route goes through north and west campus
